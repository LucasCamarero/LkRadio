# Privacy Policy for Lk Radio

Lk Radio does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app is used only to stream online radio stations.  
It does not require user accounts, logins, or personal information.

## Data usage
The app only uses:
- Internet connection to stream radio
- System audio services to play sound

No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.

## Third-party services
Radio streams are provided by external broadcasters.  
Lk Radio is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those stations.
